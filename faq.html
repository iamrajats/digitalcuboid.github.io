FAQ - Freelancers

Q: Is there any age restriction or educational qualification required to be a part of DC (Digital Cuboid)?
Answer: No, there are no age restrictions. Also, there are no qualification constraints. Anyone from anywhere can work as a freelancer at DC.
Q: What is the application process to join DC as a freelancer?
Answer: You first have to fill the "Apply as a Freelancer" application form. We will review your application and ask you to take a simple test. Also, if you can provide, we will accept your previous work sample as a content piece to judge your ability for the desired role. After this, if we find your work to be good enough, you are welcome to join us.
Q. How much will I get paid after each successful content delivery?
Answer: Before you start to work for DC, we will state a fixed price (in INR) which you will receive after every successful submission. For content writers, you can expect a price of 5p-50p/word, for graphic designers, a price of INR 50-INR 500 per poster/design and for video content creators/animators, a price of INR 100-INR 1000/video or animation. Well, after a few submissions, if we find you doing exceptionally great in your respective field of concern, rest assured to be paid well beyond these fixed price brackets. Prices may also vary from project to project, especially in the case of animators or video creators. And, after a few numbers of successful submissions, you can expect your fixed-price contract per content to increase automatically.
Q. What is the frequency of work I can expect from DC?
Answer: DC is a constantly growing network of content creators, with freelancers coming from various areas of interest and expertise. So, when it comes to project allocation, we try to match you with a one according to your areas of interest, knowledge, expertise, and previous project ratings. So, because of this process and having so many freelancers under one umbrella, it may sometimes, take some time to allocate you the work. However, when you are allocated a project, and you submit it, meeting all the expectations of the client and adhering to the deadline, this definitely will increase your chance to start receiving the steady work.
Q. What is DC's policy on plagiarism?
Answer: We have a strict anti-plagiarism policy, and we believe in the art of creating intuitive and original content. After a successful content delivery, we track your performance metrics based on a few parameters, and plagiarism is one of those parameters. The higher the percentage of plagiarized content we find, the lesser your score will be (in terms of performance metrics).
Q. What if I miss the deadline sometime?
Answer: Deadlines are not to be missed at any cost. However, if you think, a situation like that can arise, it is advised to inform us beforehand so that we can transfer your work/assignment to some other freelancer, or we can provide you with sufficient help to get it done on time. Also, missing the deadline will affect the score of your performance metrics.
Q. What kind of work/assignments can a video creator/animator expect?
Answer: 2D/3D Animations, Whiteboard Animations, Blackboard Animations, Short and Crisp Animated Films for Social Media and related usage, etc.
Q. What is the payment cycle and mode?
Answer: The billing cycle is from the 4th to 5th, i.e, for example, say, 4th March 2019 to 5th April 2019. Therefore, your payments for every cycle will be cleared after the 5th and before the 10th of every month. Mostly, the general mode of payment will be NEFT.
Q. Under what circumstances can DC terminate my contract of working as a freelancer?
Answer: Repeating cases of "poor content quality" or "plagiarized content", non-adherence to client guidelines or deadlines, and most importantly, any act which implies insincerity or cheating the company directly/indirectly in any aspect of dealing with us, will lead to immediate termination of our contract.
Q. Can I claim any right for the content I will provide for DC?
Answer: You don't get any credit for any work you do with us, apart from the monetory compensation. All rights are retained by the client of said work. You can't claim any right of any kind for any work done with us.
