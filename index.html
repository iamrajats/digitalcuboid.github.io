Content Writing

Digital Cuboid is an Integrated Content Marketplace where we excel at providing every kind of Content Writing service. We offer services at every price point for every need. More than 50% of content created isn't shared online. So, as a content writing company, we only create compelling content. Also, as far as we are concerned, we provide 100% content rights to our clients.

At Digital Cuboid, apart from the large network of freelancers as Content Writers, we also have an in-house team of content creators who work as writers, editors and managers.

Our famous Content Writing services include Web Copywriting, Website Content Writing, Social Media Content Writing, Blog Posts, Reviews, Product Descriptions, Newsletters, Taglines and Slogans, Script Writing, eBooks, Magazine Articles, Press Releases, Presentations, Business Proposals, Creative Writing- Stories & Poems, Email Writing, Editing & Proofreading, etc.

Graphic Designing

Tell us your design needs by filling out a simple form. You can send as many requests as you want & we would work on them one by one as per your priority list. Then, after we analyze the complexity of your designs, we will give you a deadline. Don't worry, you can make as many revisions as you want according to your opted subscription plan. Once the work is approved, we submit the source files in your Login portal for you to download it.

At Digital Cuboid, apart from the large network of freelancers as Graphic Designers, we also have an in-house team of content creators who are professional Graphic Designers with years of experience in the field who revise and edit the designs before every submission.

Our famous Graphic Designing services include designing Posters, Logos, Social Media Posts, Web Banners, Invites, Greeting Cards, Business Cards, Email Newsletters, Print Ads etc.

Videos

Apart from Content Writing and Graphic Designing, we also excel at Animation and Video Production. In our large network of freelancers, we have some highly skilled content creators with the professional knowledge of Photoshop, Dreamweaver, Illustration and online video tools. With the help of these freelancers, our in-house team of highly skilled & creative video creators work endlessly with an aspiration to change the face of global entertainment & marketing with the use of 2D Animation, 3D Animation and other media features.

Our video services include creating 2D/3D Animations, Whiteboard Animations, Blackboard Animations, Short and Crisp Animated Films for Social Media and related usage, Product Explainer Videos, Service Tutorial Videos, Company Culture Videos, Personalized Videos etc.
