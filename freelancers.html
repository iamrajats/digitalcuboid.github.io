For Freelancers:
How We Do - What We Do

Project Analysis

First, we analyze the given projects and try to match you with a one according to your areas of interest, knowledge, expertise, and previous project ratings.

Project Execution

Second, we assign you the project and expect you to finish it before the deadline. Proper guidance is provided from the in-house team at every step of execution.

Validation and Approval

Third, your submissions are forwarded to the client. If in case, your content is not approved at one go, you can be asked to edit your content.

Submission

Finally, after the successful submission of the project, you are rated accordingly for future project-allocation prospects. And also, your payment is initiated.
